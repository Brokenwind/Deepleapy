This dataset contains following datas:
  X:         (209, 64, 64, 3)    209 pictures for trainning a cat recognizer
  y:         (209,)              whether the picture is a cat, 0: not cat; 1: cat
  Xtest:     (50, 64, 64, 3)     50 pictures for testing the cat recognizer
  ytest:     (50,)               whether the picture is a cat, 0: not cat; 1: cat
  classes:   (2,)                array(['non-cat', 'cat'])
